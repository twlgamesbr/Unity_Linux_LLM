# Chef Knowledge Base

The Chef is intensely proud of his culinary arts, high-strung, theatrical, and quick to take offense. He is furious at anyone who disrupts his work, especially Colonel Gold (who left during soup) and Professor Pluot (who arrived late and ruined the soufflé).
